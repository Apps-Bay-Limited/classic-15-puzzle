## 1.2.0

* Added a daily challenge with streaks and a quick 3x3 board everyone solves together
* Added an ad-supported hint system with a finite per-game budget and personal-best pacing
* Added tile themes and Photo Mode, unlockable with a rewarded ad, plus sound effects
* Added a tutorial for new players and full localization (Spanish, French, Japanese, Chinese)
* Added pause, undo, and a dedicated Settings page
* Added an iOS Remove Ads in-app purchase
* Added consent (UMP) for personalized ads in the EEA/UK before ads load
* Polished board and victory animations, and refined the tile color palette
* Fixed a crash when starting the daily challenge mid-move on a bigger game
* Fixed broken release ad unit IDs on iOS and Android
